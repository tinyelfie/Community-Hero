Support

Welcome to the Nagrik Support Center. Our goal is to ensure that every user has a smooth and reliable experience while using the platform. Whether you need assistance with reporting a civic issue, managing your account, understanding platform features, or resolving a technical problem, we are here to help.

If you experience any issues while using Nagrik, we recommend first ensuring that you have a stable internet connection and are using the latest version of your web browser. Many common issues, such as failed image uploads or location detection errors, can often be resolved by refreshing the page, enabling location permissions, or trying again after a few moments.

For account-related concerns such as login problems, password recovery, profile updates, or verification issues, please contact our support team. To help us resolve your request quickly, include your registered email address and a brief description of the issue you are experiencing. We strive to respond to all support requests as promptly as possible.

If you encounter technical problems while submitting a report, such as image upload failures, map loading issues, AI analysis delays, or notification errors, please provide as much information as possible, including screenshots if available. Detailed information helps our team identify and resolve issues more efficiently.

Nagrik uses Artificial Intelligence to assist with issue classification, severity estimation, and report summarization. While we continuously work to improve the accuracy of these AI-generated results, occasional inaccuracies may occur. If you believe an AI-generated category, severity level, or summary is incorrect, you can submit feedback through the platform or contact our support team so the issue can be reviewed.

We also welcome suggestions for improving Nagrik. Feedback from citizens plays an important role in helping us enhance the platform, introduce new features, and improve the overall reporting experience. Whether you have ideas for new functionality, usability improvements, or accessibility enhancements, we encourage you to share your thoughts with us.

Our team is committed to maintaining a secure, transparent, and dependable civic reporting platform. We appreciate your patience while we investigate reported issues and work to resolve them as quickly as possible. Every report, suggestion, and piece of feedback contributes to making Nagrik a more effective platform for citizens and local authorities alike.

For any questions, technical assistance, feedback, or general inquiries, please contact the Nagrik support team using the contact information provided within the application. We are always happy to assist you and ensure that your experience with Nagrik remains smooth, reliable, and productive.
